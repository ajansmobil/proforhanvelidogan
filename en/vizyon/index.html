Our vision

To be a leading and trusted centre in cardiovascular surgery; to combine education and scientific development with regional reference care. We apply current surgical and endovascular methods in coronary, valve, aortic and congenital heart disease; TAVI, minimally invasive surgery, EVAR, TEVAR and carotid stenting are among our expertise.
