What are the stages of varicose veins?

Stages are defined by the international CEAP classification: C0 no visible signs, C1 telangiectasia/reticular, C2 varicose veins, C3 oedema, C4 skin changes (pigmentation, eczema, lipodermatosclerosis), C5 healed ulcer, C6 active ulcer. Treatment is planned according to stage; Assoc. Prof. Orhan Veli Doğan offers stage-appropriate vascular and endovascular care (EVAR, TEVAR, peripheral angioplasty).
