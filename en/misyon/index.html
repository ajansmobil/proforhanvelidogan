Our mission

Assoc. Prof. Orhan Veli Doğan and his team are committed to patient-centred, evidence-based and reliable cardiovascular surgery care. We apply current guidelines in diagnosis and treatment; we aim to be a reference centre in coronary bypass, valve surgery, TAVI and vascular and endovascular interventions. Patient safety and quality standards are our priority. For more information, see the Turkish version or contact us.
